OPPORTUNITIES

Become an Ambassador

Our EmpowHERment Ambassadors helped share our conferences with young women and non-binary folks around the world. There were two Ambassador Tracks: Internship and Fellowship. Both interns and fellows had access to exclusive leadership workshops, earned Presidential Volunteer Service Award hours, were eligible for a $100 scholarship + raffle prizes, and more! Our 163 ambassadors recruited over 35% of our total registrations.

Scholarships

Women are too often denied opportunities and support. Superposition is determined to change that narrative. We offered scholarships to support women in Social Impact & Education. Each category had 2 prizes: Gold ($250) and Silver ($150). These grants were graciously sponsored by Zagg (Social Impact) and Outschool (Scholarship).

PRIZES

Just by attending one speaker session, attendees were eligible to win these free prizes!

FAQS

Our event & prizes are 100% free.

Our EmpowHERment Conference is open to everyone, regardless of age and gender identity.

The conference will be online!

The event is scheduled to be from 10 AM PST - 4 PM PST on January 16th, 2021. Check out the schedule above for more details about specific workshops!

While it’s recommended to attend most of the conference, we do not require attending all workshops to win prizes.

Our Social Impact and Education Scholarships are available to female-identifying and non-binary individuals. However, our Education Scholarship is open only to students enrolled in any educational institution. The Ambassador Scholarship is available only to Superposition ambassadors. Applications are currently closed.

Our EmpowHERment Ambassador applications have closed, but we will be launching another ambassador program with our hackathon, Superposition V.
