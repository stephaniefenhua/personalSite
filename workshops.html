CREATING LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES



As a part of our expansion, Superposition is hosting a free virtual workshop and panel series for students of all ages and gender identities to help our community further their STEM and career development skills. So far, we have hosted 59+ workshops like High School Programs and Internships, Professional Networking, and Intro to Python with a total of 3500 attendees in 55 countries.

Our new Superposition chapters have also been leading virtual events of their own, ranging from a virtual Holiday Escape Room to a Female Founders Panel featuring 6 CEOs. View all our upcoming events on the calendar below!
